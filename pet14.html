Parrots are one of the fabulous bird on the planet.Parrots are beautiful medium-sized birds. Parrots are known as talking birds because they can imitate the human voice. Parrots eat multiple grains, fruits and vegetables, boiled rice, etc. Parrots can be very rewarding pets to the right owners, due to their intelligence and desire to interact with people. Many parrots are very affectionate, even cuddly with trusted people, and require a lot of attention from their owners constantly.
